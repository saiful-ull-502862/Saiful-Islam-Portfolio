About Me

Ph.D. candidate specializing in finite element analysis, soft materials, and biomechanics. Seeking industrial internship opportunities to apply advanced research skills in material science and numerical simulation for real-world innovation.

With over 4 years of hands-on experience in computational modeling and finite element analysis, I focus on developing multi-scale frameworks to understand complex biomechanical systems, particularly articular cartilage pathomechanics and osteoarthritis progression.

My research bridges computational modeling, bioengineering, and mechanical engineering, combining advanced FEA techniques with AI/ML approaches to solve challenging problems in biomechanics and material science.
