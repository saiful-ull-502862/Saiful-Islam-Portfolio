About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Systems Engineering with a concentration in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Louisiana at Lafayette. My research focuses on the intersection of computational mechanics and data science, specifically developing multi-scale finite element frameworks for biomechanical applications.

With a strong background in solid mechanics and numerical modeling, I leverage Python and AI/ML techniques to enhance simulation accuracy and efficiency. I am passionate about solving complex engineering problems that have real-world impact in the biomedical field.
