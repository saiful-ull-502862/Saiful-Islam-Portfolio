About Me

Ph.D. candidate specializing in Finite Element Analysis, Soft Materials, and Biomechanics. I develop multi-scale computational frameworks to solve complex challenges in osteoarthritis research.

My work integrates advanced FEA with AI/ML to bridge the gap between mechanical engineering and bioengineering. I am actively seeking industrial internship opportunities to apply these skills in real-world R&D environments.
